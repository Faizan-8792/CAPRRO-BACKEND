Accounting Intelligence

Store high-level accounting snapshots to assess audit and tax readiness. This is an intelligence layer ‚Äî not a ledger or accounting software.

What this tool actually does:

  • This tool creates a quick accounting health snapshot for a client for a specific period (for example: April 2024 or FY 2023-24).
  • It does NOT maintain books, ledgers, or entries. It only observes patterns and behaviour in accounting data.
  • It highlights early risk signals such as: irregular entries, excessive round figures, heavy month-end adjustments, or low accounting discipline.
  • The output is a simple Readiness Score (0‚Äì100) and GREEN / AMBER / RED status to help decide how smooth or difficult the upcoming audit or compliance work may be.

What this tool does NOT do:

  • It does NOT decide whether audit is applicable or mandatory. Audit applicability is governed strictly by law and turnover limits.
  • It does NOT replace professional judgement, audit procedures, or statutory verification.

Why this is useful for CA practice:

  • Helps in audit planning by identifying risky clients or periods early.
  • Saves time by indicating where deeper checking is required and where routine procedures may be sufficient.
  • Allows period-to-period comparison to see whether accounting quality is improving or deteriorating.

How to use:

  • Enter the client name and period.
  • Either select approximate indicators based on professional judgement (when books are not yet final) OR upload a CSV / Excel file if available.
  • Add remarks for any professional observation or concern.
  • Save the snapshot. Data is stored only for the selected retention period and then auto-removed.
